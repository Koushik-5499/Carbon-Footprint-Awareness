UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Alignment

EcoTrack directly contributes to the United Nations 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. By tracking emissions and adopting eco-friendly challenges, your efforts directly impact these global goals.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Target: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

EcoTrack supports SDG 13 by raising individual footprint awareness, enabling daily tracking of carbon emissions, and encouraging active habits (like reduction in transport emissions and home heating fuel) that prevent CO₂ build-up in our atmosphere.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities & Communities

Target: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

EcoTrack helps lower municipal waste, reduce reliance on private transport, and optimize clean utility consumption. By completing localized sustainability challenges, you actively lower the aggregate footprint of your local community and town.
